获奖学生代表发言

会徽设计大赛一等奖获得者 六(9)班 陆一祎

尊敬的老师们，亲爱的同学们:

大家好！

我是六(9)班的陆一祎，今天，很荣幸能够站在这里为大家介绍我设计的英语文化节节徽。

节徽整体呈圆形，象征着圆满和智慧。我的设计灵感来源于本次英语节主题——中华文化。中国传统文化源远流长，博大精深，从古到今，华夏大地涌现出了许许多多的艺术瑰宝。

大家在会徽中看到的山脉、祥云、花儿、毛笔就是代表着我们中华文化的精髓。山脉寓意着坚韧、稳固、高贵；祥云传递着积极向上的能量和思想；花儿是诗词艺术的源泉，而那支毛笔则绘画出了书中我们国土里一幅幅美丽的画面，写出了一篇篇优秀的诗歌。同学们在书的海洋中尽情遨游，欣赏着中华文化的魅力。最后和七宝鑫都的校徽元素——“鑫都”的简写字母“XD”和“麦穗”融合在一起，组成了本次英语文化节节徽。再次谢谢大家对节徽的喜欢。

通过本次丰富多彩的英语文化节活动，让我深刻地感受到了中西文化碰撞的魅力，加深了我对中华文化的理解。参加了本次英语文化节的诵读大赛和节徽的创作设计，提高了我的表达能力和文学素养，激发了我的想象力和创新力，让我在活动中找到了自信，感受到了舞台的魅力。英语学习将永无止境，让我们共同期待明年更加精彩的英语文化节！


获奖学生代表发言

高二(2)班 王亚岚

Distinguished teachers、fellow students，good morning！

I'm so honored to be here to share my feelings and experience of this year’s English Festival.I participated in the English speech competition which benefits me on two aspects,including improving my English skills as well as raising my awareness of promoting our national spirit.

On the one hand, thanks to the establishment of of this competition,contestants were given a stage to express themselves.Therefore, I gained a chance to share my opinion with others face to face by this form.Besides,during the preparation for the speech,I learned more expressions and phrases to modify my speech draft.Enrolling in the festival helps lay a solid foundation for our accomplishment of English subject.

On the other hand,speaking of the topic of the speech---”telling the story of our motherland”reminded me of several stories of our forebears fighting for the new China which fuels my commitment to make a positive impact that urges me to strive tirelessly in order to endure our nation”s culture and custom.

Fellow students,this is our era and it’s high time for us to make a difference.Your involvement of school’s English festival may be the very first step of moving forward in our life.It doesn't matter whether or not you have participated in this year’s activities.As long as you’re bold enough to try,it will definitely be a rewarding experience which helps us To grow.So why not take action and get ready for next year’s Festival?I’ll be looking forward for your shiny showing up.

Thank you!
